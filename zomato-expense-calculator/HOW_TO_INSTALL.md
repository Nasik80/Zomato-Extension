# How to Install Zomato Expense Calculator

1.  **Open Chrome Extensions Page**:
    -   Type `chrome://extensions` in your address bar and press Enter.

2.  **Enable Developer Mode**:
    -   Toggle the switch in the top-right corner of the page to "ON".

3.  **Load the Extension**:
    -   Click the "Load unpacked" button in the top-left corner.
    -   Navigate to the folder `zomato-expense-calculator` inside `Zomato Extension`.
    -   Click "Select" or "Open".

4.  **Use the Extension**:
    -   **Important**: Log in to [Zomato.com](https://www.zomato.com) first.
    -   Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar.
    -   Find "Zomato Food Expense Calculator" and pin it if you like.
    -   Click the icon and select "View Dashboard".
    -   In the dashboard, click "Sync Data" to fetch your order history.

## Troubleshooting
-   If "Sync Data" fails, ensure you are logged into Zomato in another tab.
-   If it still fails, check the Console (Right-click > Inspect > Console) for error messages.
